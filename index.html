about

who is deathproof audio?

Hey there, I'm Deathproof Audio, but you can call me Chris Derry!

I'm a solo DJ based in the vibrant town of Swindon, UK. If you're looking for a musical adventure that'll make you groove, I've got you covered. I'm all about bringing the energy and keeping the beats pumping!

My style is a wild mix of psydub, drum and bass, jungle, and anything that gets your body moving. I love blending genres and creating a sound that's truly unique. From mind-bending basslines to infectious rhythms, my sets are designed to take you on a thrilling ride.

Music is my passion, and you can feel it in every beat I drop. When I'm behind the decks, I'm in my element, pouring my heart and soul into the music. The dance floor becomes a playground of euphoria as we connect through the power of sound.

I've been making waves in the underground scene, playing at various venues and events. The energy I bring to the stage is contagious, and I thrive on creating an unforgettable experience for everyone in the room. It's all about that magical moment when the crowd and the music become one.

But it doesn't stop there. I'm also a studio junkie, constantly cooking up fresh tracks that pack a punch. I'm all about big sounds and fat beats that get your body pulsating. When I'm not rocking the dance floor, you'll find me in my studio, pushing the boundaries of sound and crafting sonic landscapes that transport you to another dimension.

I'm on a mission to spread my love for music far and wide. Join me on this exhilarating journey as we create unforgettable memories and celebrate the power of music together. Let's make some noise!
